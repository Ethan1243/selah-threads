02 / Design Anatomy

Why Every Detail Matters

Nothing on this shirt is accidental. Each element serves both an aesthetic and a spiritual purpose.

01
The Tree
Hand-drawn with organic, imperfect lines. Not clipart, not AI-generated. The slightly rough strokes give it warmth and humanity — this is a tree that was planted, not manufactured.
02
The Hidden Cross
The root system forms a cross shape that becomes visible only when you look for it. The wearer knows it's there. It's a personal anchor — faith that runs beneath the surface.
03
The Scripture
"Jer. 17:8" runs in tiny text along the trunk — not across the chest. It's a reference, not a billboard. Those who notice will look it up. Those who don't will still see a beautiful design.
04
Single-Color Print
Cream ink on pigment-dyed cotton creates a worn, tonal look from day one. The design feels like it's part of the shirt, not sitting on top of it. This also keeps production costs down.
05
Olive Branch Color
The primary colorway uses Olive Branch green — symbolizing peace and renewal. It also stands out in a market saturated with black and white Christian tees.
06
Relaxed Fit
A slightly oversized, relaxed silhouette that aligns with the 2026 streetwear trend. Comfortable enough to sleep in, sharp enough to wear out. Faith should feel easy to carry.

03 / Materials

What It's Made Of

Every material was chosen for a reason. Here's the full bill of materials for one "Rooted" t-shirt.

Material Specification Purpose
Blank T-Shirt Core 100% combed ring-spun cotton, 240 GSM heavyweight, pigment-dyed Thick, soft, vintage feel. Holds shape after washing. Pigment dye gives each shirt a unique lived-in look.
Screen Print Ink Design Water-based discharge ink, cream/off-white Bonds with the fabric instead of sitting on top. Softer hand-feel than plastisol. Better for the vintage aesthetic.
Screen 230 mesh count aluminum frame High mesh count captures the fine detail in the branches and root work. One-time cost amortized across the run.
Neck Label Branding Woven damask label, tagless heat transfer option Custom "Vesper Threads" branding. Tagless option for maximum comfort — no scratchy tag.
Hang Tag Recycled kraft cardstock, 350 GSM, cotton twine Carries the verse (Jeremiah 17:8) and brand story. Kraft material aligns with earth-tone aesthetic.
Packaging Recycled kraft mailer or compostable poly bag Eco-friendly packaging that reinforces brand values. Protects product during shipping.
Thread Tonal cotton thread, matched to garment color Clean, professional finish with no visible contrast stitching.

05 / Why This Design

Why "Rooted" First?

Of all the designs in the Vesper Collection, "Rooted" is the strongest lead product for three reasons:

It's universally wearable. The tree design appeals to anyone — it reads as nature art to some, faith statement to others. It doesn't alienate. It invites.

It's production-efficient. Single-color screen print on one side keeps costs low and quality high. No complex registration, no multi-pass printing. Clean and repeatable.

It tells a layered story. Surface level: beautiful tree. Second look: the roots are a cross. Third look: there's a verse reference. Each layer rewards attention — exactly the kind of depth that builds word-of-mouth.
