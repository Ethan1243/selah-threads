# Vesper Threads

**Worn in faith. Rooted in purpose.**

Vesper Threads is a faith-based clothing brand rooted in comfort, warmth, and faith. The name comes from the Latin word *vesper*, meaning evening prayer — a quiet, reflective moment of devotion at the close of day.

## The "Rooted" Design

The debut t-shirt features a hand-drawn tree whose roots form the subtle shape of a cross. The Scripture reference *Jeremiah 17:8* runs in small text along the trunk. It's faith hidden in plain sight — visible to those who look closely, personal to those who wear it.

> "Blessed is the one who trusts in the Lord... They will be like a tree planted by the water that sends out its roots by the stream." — Jeremiah 17:7-8

## Brand Details

- **Audience:** General Christian — all ages, unisex
- **Style:** Earth-toned, vintage wash, comfort-first
- **Fabric:** 100% combed ring-spun cotton, 240 GSM heavyweight, pigment-dyed
- **Print:** Single-color water-based discharge ink (cream)

- **Sizes:** S — 3XL (relaxed fit)
